Welcome to Big John Nolt's BBQ and Catering

Big John Nolt's BBQ & Catering is a full service off-site caterer that keeps the "home-cooked from scratch" flavor in our foods. With over 20 years of catering experience in Central Pennsylvania and beyond, you can relax knowing that we set up, serve and clean up. From picnics, private parties, corporate events and weddings, you can trust our experienced and professional staff to provide a delicious mouth-watering feast for you and your guests.

Big John Nolt's BBQ & Catering offers a variety of menu options and will be happy to create custom menus for your event, budget and lifestyle.
